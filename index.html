Intro

Datashark is a marketplace where consumers can sell their data to data-driven companies. Machine learning is the future, and every startup, student and savant in Silicon Valley wants to create the next big data-driven project. But all of these projects get stuck in a negative feedback loop; in order to get data they need users, in order to get users they need a product, but in order to make a product they need data.

Not the bloated, expensive datasets they currently buy for over $2000 per user per year. They need highly granular, verified data -and we’re going to give it to them. Our users grant us access to their Facebook likes, Google location history, who they follow on Instagram, etc. With this opt-in data, we can help our users profit off their data while saving companies money on these data deals.

With our seamless, end-to-end architecture, all the user needs to do is give permission to our mobile app, and the data is automatically backed up, ready to be purchased by a data company. When the transaction finally happens, the cash is automatically deposited into the user’s account, while the business gets instant access to the data they so desperately need.
